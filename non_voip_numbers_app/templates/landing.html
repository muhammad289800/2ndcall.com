How A2P Compliance Works on 2ndCall

Carriers require every business that sends SMS in the US to be individually registered. We make that automatic.

1️⃣

Business Verification

Sign up with your legal business name, EIN/tax ID, website, and declared messaging use case. We submit your Brand to The Campaign Registry.

2️⃣

Campaign Approval

Describe what you send (notifications, 2FA, marketing) with sample messages and opt-in flow. Carriers approve within 1–5 business days.

3️⃣

Send on Your Own Brand

Once approved, messages go out on numbers tied to your registered brand — never a shared or umbrella sender. STOP/HELP handled automatically.

Read the SMS Policy and Acceptable Use Policy for prohibited content (SHAFT, lead-gen, loan-sharking, etc.). Violations result in suspension without refund.
